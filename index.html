ABOUT US

Welcome to Simon Munyoki Tiles & Terrazzo

At Simon Munyoki Tiles & Terrazzo we work with clients to create aesthetically pleasing projects and spaces. Clients range from homeowners to large corporations, and the spaces designed are equally varied, ranging from simple indoor and outdoor home environments to Offices, Hotel lobbies and lavish mansions. Simon Munyoki Tiles & Terrazzo, we specialize in Tiles & Terrazzo interior spaces for commercial, retail, hospitality and residential sectors amongst other major and minor renovations. Our emphasis on detail and passion for quality material at competitive prices provides a complete, tailored service covering each and every aspect of any project’s requirements.

GOALS AND OBJECTIVES.

Simon Munyoki Tiles & Terrazzo works to create spaces that are attractive, functional, and safe while meeting the specific needs of our clients. While working with our clients, we strive to achieve our goals and objectives through: -
-Communicating with clients to understand their style, wants and budget
-Developing a design plan, blueprints and budget estimate.
-Working with building inspectors to ensure plans are up to code.
